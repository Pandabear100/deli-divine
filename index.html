About

DELI divine offers a range of the finest gourmet meats (natural and smoked), cheese (local and imported) and antipasto products and a selection of some of the most delicious and decadent preserves, cooking ingredients and accompaniments that you’re ever likely to see.

DELI divine has quite a reputation as a knowledgeable and respected cheesemonger. Perth’s cheese aficionados are drawn to the diversity of our national and international range as well as the useful advice and accompaniment selections available to true cheese lovers!

From family table favourites to gourmet delicacies, DELI divine invites you to indulge your senses - affordable luxuries in the heart of Subiaco from some of Australia’s, and the world’s, most reputable providores.

The Cheese Experts

More than simply purveyors of fine local and imported cheeses, DELI divine is a cheesemonger whose engaging staff can offer expert advice on cheese board combinations and accompaniments to your selections - enhancing the distinctive textures and flavours of the some of the world's finest cheese products.

We've tasted them all and we've learned from the makers (and from hands-on experience) just what accompaniments complement which cheese varieties. We can also advise on storage, 'shelf life' and how to know whether a cheese is 'ripe' or not. (And even whether you can eat the rind on some cheeses or not - believe it or not, in some cases you can!)

DELI divine stocks cows' milk, goats' milk, sheeps' milk and buffalo milk cheeses - we cater to every palate, whether you are seeking a robust Italian cow's milk cheese or a delightful French soft brie that goes perfectly with the Cab Sav you've been saving for a special occasion!

We also create spectacularly tasty cheese gift boxes for true connoisseurs - ask in-store to ensure availability of your selections.
